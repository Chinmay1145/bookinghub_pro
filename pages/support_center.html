Frequently Asked Questions

You can modify your booking dates through your dashboard or by using our quick modification tool. Simply enter your booking reference, select new dates, and we'll show you availability and any price differences. Most modifications can be completed instantly, though some may require confirmation from the hotel or car rental company.

Our cancellation policy varies by booking type and provider. Generally:

  • • Hotels: Free cancellation up to 24-48 hours before check-in
  • • Car Rentals: Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup
  • • Package Deals: Cancellation terms apply to individual components

Specific terms are always shown during booking and in your confirmation email.

Package deals combine hotel and car rental bookings for additional savings and convenience. When you book both together, you'll receive a bundled discount and coordinated arrangements. Your car will be ready when you arrive, and hotels are notified of your expected arrival time. Package modifications affect both components, and cancellations follow the most restrictive policy of the included services.

Yes, we partner with leading insurance providers to offer comprehensive travel protection. Coverage includes trip cancellation, medical emergencies, baggage loss, and travel delays. Insurance can be added during booking or within 24 hours of your initial reservation. We offer different coverage levels to match your needs and budget.
